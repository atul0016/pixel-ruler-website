Privacy Policy

Pixel Ruler + Alignment Checker • Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the "Pixel Ruler + Alignment Checker" Chrome Extension (the "Extension"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Extension is designed to function solely on your local device.

2. Data Collection & Use

Pixel Ruler operates entirely locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or analytics. The Extension only interacts with the current webpage to overlay measurement rulers via standard browser APIs.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing activity is tracked
  • No analytics or telemetry data is sent
  • All preferences are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local

3. Browser Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions, each for a specific functional purpose:

  • activeTab — To inject the ruler overlay on the current page when you activate it
  • storage — To save your settings (colors, grid size) locally on your device
  • scripting — To programmatically inject the ruler content script into tabs
  • contextMenus — To add right-click menu options for quick ruler controls
  • notifications — To show status notifications (e.g., installation success)

4. Payment Processing

If you choose to purchase premium features, payments are processed securely through our third-party payment providers:

  • PayPal — For international payments (credit/debit cards, PayPal balance)
  • Razorpay — For India-based payments (UPI, cards, netbanking)

We do not store your credit card details or personal financial information. Payment verification is handled by encrypted serverless functions. Only a non-reversible activation token is stored locally to unlock premium features.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension connects to the following external services only during the premium purchase process:

  • Our payment backend (rulerguide.netlify.app) — For payment verification only
  • PayPal SDK — Only loaded on the payment page
  • Razorpay SDK — Only loaded on the payment page

No data is transmitted during normal extension usage.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to retain or delete. All user preferences remain in your browser's local storage and are removed when you uninstall the Extension.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. Since no personal data is collected at all, the Extension is compliant with COPPA regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes that affect how your data is handled will be communicated through the Extension update notes.

9. Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR — European General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA — California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Chrome Web Store — Developer Program Policies
  • COPPA — Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the Extension, please contact us:

  • Via the Chrome Web Store support tab for this Extension
  • Via our GitHub profile
