Privacy Policy

Advanced Smart Capture — Screenshot, OCR & Annotate • Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the "Advanced Smart Capture" Chrome Extension (the "Extension"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Extension is designed to function strictly locally on your device.

2. Data Collection & Use

Smart Capture operates entirely locally. All screenshots, recordings, annotations, OCR processing, and editing happen on your machine. We do NOT upload, collect, or transmit any of your captures, images, or browsing data to any external servers.

  • No screenshots or captures are sent to external servers
  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing activity is tracked
  • No analytics or telemetry data is sent
  • OCR text extraction runs locally (no cloud processing)
  • Capture history is stored locally in your browser's storage

3. Browser Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions for specific functional purposes:

  • activeTab / tabs — To access the current page for screenshot capture
  • storage / unlimitedStorage — To save capture history and settings locally
  • scripting — To inject the capture overlay and selection tools
  • downloads — To save captured images and PDFs to your downloads folder
  • contextMenus — To add right-click menu options for quick capture
  • alarms — To support scheduled capture functionality
  • debugger — To access the Chrome DevTools protocol for high-quality full-page captures

4. Payment Processing

If you choose to purchase premium features, payments are processed securely through our third-party payment providers:

  • PayPal — For international payments (credit/debit cards, PayPal balance)
  • Razorpay — For India-based payments (UPI, cards, netbanking)

We do not store your credit card details or personal financial information. Payment verification is handled by encrypted serverless functions. Only a non-reversible activation token is stored locally to unlock premium features.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension connects to external services only during the premium purchase process:

  • Our payment backend — For payment verification only
  • PayPal SDK — Only loaded on the payment page
  • Razorpay SDK — Only loaded on the payment page

No data is transmitted during normal capture, annotation, or OCR usage.

6. Local Storage

The Extension stores the following data locally on your device:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Capture history (thumbnails and metadata)
  • Premium activation status

All local data is removed when you uninstall the Extension.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected, the Extension is COPPA compliant.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

9. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • GDPR — European General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA — California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Chrome Web Store — Developer Program Policies
  • COPPA — Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

10. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab or our GitHub profile.
