Privacy Policy for Pixel Ruler

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the "Pixel Ruler" Chrome Extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This extension is designed to function solely on your local device.

2. Data Collection & Use

Pixel Ruler operates locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or analytics. The tool only interact with the current webpage to overlay a measurement ruler via standard browser APIs.

3. Payment Processing

If you choose to purchase premium features, payments are processed securely through our third-party payment providers (Stripe/PayPal/Razorpay). We do not store your credit card details or personal financial information on our servers.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
