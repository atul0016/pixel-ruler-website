Privacy Policy for Smart Capture

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the "Smart Capture" Chrome Extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This extension is designed to function strictly locally on your device.

2. Data Collection & Permissions

Smart Capture requires permissions to capture screenshots and recordings of your screen. All capturing, processing, and editing occurs locally on your machine. We do NOT upload, collect, or transmit any of your screenshots, recordings, or browsing data to any external servers.

3. Payment Processing

If you choose to unlock premium features, your purchase is processed securely via third-party payment gateways (Stripe/PayPal/Razorpay). We do not have access to or store your sensitive payment information.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will be published here.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or require support, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
